Welcome to Ushelf

Ushelf is a digital platform designed to help users organize their books and files as they would on a physical shelf at home or in the office. With Ushelf, you can upload files, arrange them digitally, and invite others to view and access your shelves. The platform ensures the safety and accessibility of your files from any device, anywhere.

About Me

My name is Mary Mengistu, and I am currently a student at Jimma University, also taking an ALX Software Engineering course. This project is part of my portfolio for the ALX course.

Through Ushelf, I aimed to address the challenge of organizing digital files effectively, inspired by my personal experiences with physical file management. Despite the complexities encountered during development, this project has been a valuable learning experience, showcasing my skills and dedication.
